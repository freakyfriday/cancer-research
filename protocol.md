# Interactions & Contraindications

## ❌ Hard Contraindications

| Interaction | Risk |
|---|---|
| **Methylene Blue + SSRIs** | Serotonin syndrome — potentially fatal. |
| **Methylene Blue + G6PD deficiency** | Haemolytic anaemia. G6PD test mandatory. |
| **IV Vitamin C + G6PD deficiency** | Haemolytic anaemia. G6PD test mandatory. |
| **Red Light directly over tumour (without MB)** | May stimulate tumour growth. Use only with MB as photosensitiser. |

## ⚠️ Timing Separations Required

| Pair | Rule | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **D-serine ↔ Oral Vit C** | D-serine ≥3 hrs before Vit C | Let NADPH depletion establish before pro-oxidant strike |
| **D-serine ↔ Food** | Take on empty stomach with Serrapeptase | Avoid L-serine competition from dietary amino acids |
| **Serrapeptase / Nattovena** | Empty stomach — 30 min before food | With food they digest food proteins instead of absorbing systemically |
| **Molybdenum ↔ Quercetin** | Different meals (morning vs lunch) | Quercetin inhibits xanthine oxidase (Mo-dependent enzyme) |
| **Zinc ↔ Magnesium** | 6+ hrs apart (lunch vs night) | Compete for absorption at high doses |
| **IV Vit C ↔ IV Glutathione** | ≥24 hrs apart (separate days) | Glutathione neutralises Vit C's pro-oxidant cancer kill |
| **IV Vit C ↔ Chemo** | 24–48 hrs apart | Coordinate with oncologist |
| **Sauna ↔ Night medications** | 2–3 hr gap | Sweating may reduce drug absorption |
| **EGCG** | Empty/light stomach preferred | Heavy meals reduce EGCG uptake |

## ⚠️ Monitoring Required

| Item | What to Monitor |
|---|---|
| **Serrapeptase + Nattovena** | Blood-thinning — stop both 2 weeks pre-surgery. Avoid with anticoagulants. |
| **Anthogenol** | Enhances anticoagulant effects. Monitor if on blood thinners. |
| **Quercetin + Resveratrol** | Both inhibit CYP3A4/CYP2C19 → may ↑ blood levels of other drugs. |
| **Chlorella + Platinum chemo** | Chelation may bind cisplatin/carboplatin. **Stop Chlorella during platinum chemo.** |
| **EGCG ≥600 mg/day** | Monitor liver enzymes. |
| **Molybdenum** | May lower copper over time — beneficial (anti-angiogenic) but monitor periodically. |
| **Ivermectin 120 mg/day** | Well above standard dose. Fat co-admin essential. Medical supervision required. |
| **Melatonin** | Protocol: 3 mg. Cancer trials: 20 mg/day. Discuss escalation with physician. |
| **Serrapeptase + Drug absorption** | May ↑ blood levels of co-administered drugs by improving circulation. |
| **Resveratrol >1 g/day** | GI discomfort; CYP3A4 effects increase. |
| **D-serine** | Renally excreted — monitor creatinine + eGFR at each blood draw. If eGFR < 60, reduce or stop. Ramp: 1 g wk 1 → 1.5 g wk 2 → 2–2.5 g wk 3. |
